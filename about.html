About Me

Outside of my working life, sometings I enjoy that are the aformentioned photography, cars, legos, and video games.

Photography

As stated before, I have been doing photography since high school and it has been a constant in my life since. I love taking pictures of landscapes, macro subjects, and I love photographing candid moments.

Cars

My love of automobiles came around when my best friend in high school bought a 2018 Toyota Corolla, and I thought was the coolest car to exist. Since then I have been down a rabbit hole and now own my very own 1988 BMW 325is that I spend money, time, and effort into keeping running. While it is one of my most favorite cars ever designed, I do have a love/hate relationship with it- I love to work on it, hate to break it. However, it gives me experiences I cannot replace.

Legos

I have been building legos since I was a young child, some of my favorite memories are with Legos and the varioius sets that have been out on the market. My favorite right now is the new Keith Harring set. I love Keith Harring's work and have a tattoo of one of his pieces.

Video Games

Video games are a big part of my life, while I don't spend every waking hour playing them, I have wonderful memories with them. Whether its playing with friends late at night or a casual playthrough of my favorites, and as cheesey as it may be, I will always have time to for a game.
